About

SF Event Photo is a boutique event photography agency built for speed, quality, and discretion. We partner with brands, agencies, and production companies to document product launches, conferences, brand activations, and executive gatherings across San Francisco, Los Angeles, and beyond. Our approach is simple: show up prepared, stay invisible, deliver fast. Same-day edits when you need them. A curated gallery by morning. No watermarks on your proofs. Just clean, editorial images ready for press, social, or your internal recap—often before your attendees have left the building.
