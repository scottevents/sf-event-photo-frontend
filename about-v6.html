About the Agency

SF Event Photo is a boutique event photography agency that partners with brands, agencies, and production companies to document product launches, conferences, brand activations, and executive gatherings across San Francisco, Los Angeles, and beyond.

Our approach is simple: show up prepared, stay invisible, deliver fast. Same-day edits when you need them. A curated gallery by morning. No watermarks on your proofs. Just clean, editorial images ready for press, social, or your internal recap—often before your attendees have left the building.

We work with lean teams, move quickly, and treat every event with the same level of care whether it is a two-hundred person product launch or an intimate dinner for twelve.
