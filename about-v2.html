About

SF Event Photo is a boutique event photography agency built for speed, quality, and discretion. We partner with the world's most recognized brands to document the moments that define their events, delivering editorial-grade images on timelines that match the pace of modern marketing.

Approach

We built this agency on a single belief: the best event photography is invisible. Our photographers understand the room before they enter it. They anticipate the handshake, the keynote reaction, the unscripted moment between sessions that tells the real story of your event.

Every assignment starts with a detailed brief and ends with a polished gallery delivered on your timeline. No watermarks on your proofs. No unnecessary back-and-forth. Just clean, editorial images ready for press, social, or your internal recap, often before your attendees have left the building.

We work with lean teams, move quickly, and treat every event with the same level of care whether it is a two-hundred person product launch at the Moscone Center or an intimate dinner for twelve at a private residence in Pacific Heights.
